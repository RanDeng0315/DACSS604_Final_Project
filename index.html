Research Project: Optimizing Warehousing and Logistics

My project focuses on helping small-scale entrepreneurs in the e-commerce industry optimize warehousing and logistics strategies. By analyzing real-world data such as Logistics Managers Index (LMI), regional order demand, and transportation costs, the project identifies cost-effective and efficient solutions for warehousing and delivery.

Key insights include trends in logistics performance, regional differences in order demand, and a comparison of transportation costs across different shipping modes. These findings aim to help small businesses make data-driven decisions to minimize costs and maximize efficiency.
